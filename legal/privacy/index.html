4. Information we collect

4.1 Information you provide

The App stores certain preferences on your device using iOS local storage (UserDefaults). This may include:

  • Favorite team / nation selection (“My Team”) — to personalize fixtures and related content
  • Onboarding completion flags — to avoid repeating first-run flows
  • Analytics consent choice — whether you opted in or out of optional analytics

We do not operate user account registration or login in the current version of the App.

4.2 Information collected automatically (optional analytics only)

If you opt in to analytics in the App (via the consent sheet and/or Settings), we use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect limited usage and diagnostic information, such as:

  • App interactions and feature usage events
  • General device/app information (e.g. device model, OS version, app version)
  • Approximate region or language settings derived from device configuration

If you do not opt in, Firebase Analytics is not used for your session. Core App features remain available without analytics consent.

In our Firebase configuration, advertising-related storage and ad personalization are disabled. Analytics data is used to understand how features are used, not for cross-app advertising.

4.3 Subscription and purchase information

goallll Premium is an auto-renewable subscription sold through Apple’s App Store. Apple processes payments; we do not receive or store your payment card details.

We may receive subscription entitlement/status information from Apple (via StoreKit) to determine whether you have access to Premium features — for example, whether a subscription or free trial is active.

  • Product ID: com.worldcup.damas.football.premium.monthly
  • Display price: typically $1.99/month (may vary by storefront and local taxes)
  • Free trial: 3-day free trial for eligible new subscribers, where offered by Apple

4.4 Sports data requests

To show live scores, fixtures, standings, lineups, and related tournament information, the App requests data from a third-party sports data API (e.g. API-Football or an equivalent provider). These requests generally relate to match and tournament data, not your personal identity. Standard network information (such as IP address) may be processed by the provider as part of delivering API responses.

4.5 What we do not collect (current implementation)

As of the last updated date above, the App does not:

  • Send push notifications (feature disabled in code)
  • Request access to location, contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • Use the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework for cross-app tracking
  • Operate social login or user profile accounts

5. How we use information

We use information as follows:

  • Provide the App — display scores, schedules, groups, match previews, and personalized team content
  • Remember preferences — store your team selection, onboarding state, and analytics consent on device
  • Manage subscriptions — verify Premium access through Apple
  • Improve the App (optional) — if you opt in, analyze aggregated usage via Firebase Analytics
  • Support and compliance — respond to inquiries and meet legal obligations

We do not use your information for cross-app advertising or to sell personal data.
